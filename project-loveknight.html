Story

The Dunjunion

Sir Lambertus, a valiant knight, is still stuck in a run in a roguelike. He has been stuck in the Dunjunion for 6 years and has yet to defeat the boss, El Rey Cristobal Von Strativosky. Over the years, he begins to develop feelings towards his favorite merchant, Lemarchand. It's then that Eden begins to become interested in Lambertus' crush. Our brave knight therefore has a new objective: to seduce the one he loves thanks to the Power of Love before his rival steals it from him.

TileSet

Tilemap Design

I designed all tilemaps for the dungeon environment — floors, walls, and a full layout reference for the Game Designer to implement the level correctly.

UI Design

Menu Icons & Buttons

I created all the game's UI icons — parameters, menus, credits, cancel, play, next level, accept, pause, and retry — ensuring a cohesive visual language throughout the mobile interface.

VFX

Particle Effects

I designed the game's key particle effects — dust, heart, and magic trail — to reinforce the game's romantic and whimsical tone while giving clear visual feedback to the player.
