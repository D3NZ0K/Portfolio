Gameplay

How to Play

Slip on your slippers and strap on your lizard tail to control your Kaiju and destroy the city! The physical controller tracks your real movements to unleash destruction in the game.

Core Mechanic

Building Destruction with Cell Fracture

I created the building destruction using a Blender add-on called Cell Fracture, which allows me to randomly separate different parts of a building. Then, I enabled Rigid Body on the fragments to simulate the collapse of the buildings and make the destruction effect more realistic.

Environment

Urban City Asset Pack

Here is a selection of assets I created to decorate an urban city environment — all modeled and textured to maintain a consistent low-poly aesthetic readable at the scale of a giant Kaiju.
