Character Animation

Cat Animations with Blender

During this project I took care of the integrity of the cat animations with Blender, covering the full range of gameplay states — from idle and walk cycles to combat moves and reactions.

3D Modeling

Trap Modeling with Blender & 3DS Max

I modeled the full set of in-game traps — each designed to fit the arena's chaotic energy while remaining readable during gameplay.

VFX

Particle Systems & VFX with Unity

I also created particle systems and VFX in Unity to give life to the traps and power-ups, enhancing visual feedback for the player.

Bonus — Stand & Merch

Stickers, Team Logo & 3D-printed Trophies

For the game stand at the school showcase, I created stickers, the team logo, and two trophies produced with a 3D printer — turning the booth into a fully branded experience.
