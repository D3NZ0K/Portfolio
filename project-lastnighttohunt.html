Context

A Werewolf's Revenge

The Last Night to Hunt is a project realized in a school setting over one month. During the day, you are in human form and must investigate to discover different quests. Be careful to remain discreet — although you are dangerous in wolf form, you remain vulnerable during the day.

At night, you turn into a werewolf. You will have two different ways to reach your target. The actions you take during the day will play a crucial role in your success.

Character Animation

Werewolf Animations

Characters & Creatures
