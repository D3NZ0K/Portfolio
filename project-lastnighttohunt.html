First School Project

Context

A Werewolf's Revenge

The Last Night to Hunt is a project realized in a school setting over a few months. During the day, you are in human form and must investigate to discover different quests to help you in your final task of assassination. Be careful to remain discreet — although you are dangerous in wolf form, you remain vulnerable during the day.

At night, you turn into a werewolf. You will have two different ways to reach your target. The actions you take during the day will play a crucial role in your success and in the way you approach your target.

Character Animations

I animated the full character state machine — covering all movement and combat states needed to bring the werewolf protagonist to life.

Other 3D Meshes
